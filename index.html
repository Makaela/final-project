Cyber Bullying

What is it?
The use of electronic communication device to taughnt and insult a person.
Typically sending messages over social media, these messages are usually intimidating or threatening.

How to identify it?
Usually the child or person being cyberbullied will be too ashamed to tell someone they are being bullied.
That's why if an allogation is made an ivestigation must be done to collect all evidence.

How to prevent it?
Restrict access on social media, or monitor your child's activity on social media.
Also, prolonging or preventing your child's access to technology.
Such as computers, and cellular devices will make cyberbullying impossibletowards your child, or prevent them from bullying other kids.
